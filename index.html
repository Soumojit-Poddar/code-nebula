About MoodVibe

Your companion for mental wellness

MoodVibe is a mental health companion designed to help you track your moods, discover activities that improve your well-being, and develop healthy habits for your mind and body.

Our mission is to make mental health care accessible and engaging. Whether you're feeling happy, anxious, or somewhere in between, MoodVibe provides personalized tools and resources to support your emotional well-being.
